Choose to Deepen your Relationship

Choosing to deepend your relationship with Jesus Christ is a choice. We know that our ultimate goal is to live with our Heavenly Father again and we can only do that through the covenants we make in the temple. We've seen the purpose for teaching seminary and as we make sure we are focused on the purpose, then we will consequently make changes in our lives to deepend our relationship with Jesus Christ.

Christ is the Center of our Lives

We need to make sure Christ and his teachings are the center of our lives. The best way we can get to know Him is by learning and studying his teachings. Doing so will make you a better seminary teacher and more importantly a better disciple of Jesus Christ.

Be in the Temple

We need to strive to spend time in the temple. This will allow us to make and keep our covenants we have made. This is crucuial to deepening our relationship with Christ.

Eternal Families

We need to have our sights on what matters and that is living with our families forever. We need to prepare ourselves for this true possibility so that we can teach the students in a way that conencts them to Him. Having this as a focus will help deepen a relationship with Jesus Christ.
